You should be moving more

A small increase in daily activity levels can have drastic improvements to overall health, and almost everyone should be moving more. The benefits far outweigh the negatives.

Different ways being healthy will improve your life

Frequent exercise reduces cortisol and helps to regulate your mood, even proving to aid in mental disorders such as depression & anxiety

Doing cardio often will improve the heart's overall ability to function, and a healthy heart is important for preventing ailments such as heart disease and cardiac arrest

Maintaining a mostly healthy diet will have significant benefits to mitigating negative emotions, reducing body fat percentage, helping the skin, improving gut health, and a myriad of other things

Being consistent with working out and eating a healthy diet, will improve one's physique. Havinga a nice, healthy-looking physique can aid tremendously in one's mental health, specifically to one's self-image
